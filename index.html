Help

A top down view of the sun and two planets is shown in the upper window, and the appearance of the target planet from the observer planet is shown in the bottom window.

Use the planet selection dropdowns on the right to simulate views from different planets in our solar system, or use the sliders to select arbitrary distances from the sun.

Run the simulation using the Play / Pause button, or click and drag the planets into different positions.
